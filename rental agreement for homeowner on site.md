##CLEANING

All roommates agree to share the responsibilities of cleaning and maintenance of the Rental as well as the shared space, which includes the kitchen, living room, 1st floor and 2nd floor bathrooms (but not the bathroom adjoining the master bedroom). Cleaning may include dusting, vacuuming, emptying trash, mopping floors, and wiping down countertop and other surfaces.

##HOUSE RULES

A) Smoking & drug use is not allowed on the Rental, including the bedroom occupied by Renter.

B) No pets are allowed on the Rental.

C) Notice to Owner/ Agent is necessary for parties, gatherings, or celebrations.

D) Notice to Owner/ Agent is necessary for guests.

E) Quiet hours are from 11 pm – 8 am.

##DAMAGES

Renter, identified above, shall at all times maintain the Rental in a clean and sanitary condition and in the same condition as they were at the start of rental, reasonable use and wear excepted. For any maintenance or repair of the Rental, Renter shall notify the Owner. If Renter fails to notify the Owner of maintenance or repairs, as agreed, the Owner shall have the option to make such repairs.  Renter agrees to pay for the cost of repairs and maintenance from damages s/he causes and will owe the amount due by the time next month’s rent is due. Renter shall not paint or wallpaper any part of the Rental nor shall Renter make any interior or exterior alteration or change in the Rental nor shall Renter change any lock or re-key any lock. Renter shall not install any washing machine, dryer, air conditioner, space heater, waterbed, or fixture. No object shall be thrown from any porch, balcony or window nor kept on any windowsill. Renter shall be liable for any misuse of any plumbing fixture or equipment, including disposal or garbage that damages any fixture or clogs any pipe. At the termination of the Roommate Agreement, Renter shall surrender the Rental with all keys to the Homeowners in the same condition as they now are, reasonable use and wear excepted. Should Renter fail to turn over all keys at the end of the rental period, the Homeowners shall be permitted to replace the locks and keys immediately at Renter’s sole cost and expense. Renter shall be responsible for all damage or loss caused to the Rental during the tenancy, whether by Renter or by any invitee or guest of Renter, excluding acts of God or any injury or loss caused by the Homeowners or for which the Homeowners are statutorily liable.
